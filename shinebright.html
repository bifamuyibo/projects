ShineBright Cleaning, 2025

Role: UX/UI Design, Branding, Web Development
Deliverables: Website Redesign, Booking Form, Wireframes, User Flows

View Live Project

ShineBright Cleaning is a professional cleaning service that wanted to modernize its online presence. The main challenge was creating a booking system that was simple, fast, and trustworthy for users while also showcasing ShineBright’s brand identity.

Problem

The original ShineBright website was outdated, lacked trust signals, and had no integrated booking form. Customers often abandoned inquiries due to unclear pricing and a long back-and-forth process.

Solution

I redesigned ShineBright’s website to focus on clarity, simplicity, and conversion. The centerpiece is a booking form where customers can quickly choose services, schedule appointments, and receive confirmation in minutes.

Features

  • Streamlined Booking: Easy-to-use form with service selection and time slots.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
  • Trust Elements: Testimonials, certifications, and clear pricing tables.
  • Brand Refresh: Clean, modern visuals aligned with ShineBright’s professional image.

Impact

The redesign reduced booking friction, improved trust, and positioned ShineBright as a professional, customer-friendly cleaning brand. Users reported that the new form was significantly easier to use, leading to more completed bookings.
