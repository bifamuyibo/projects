Role: UX/UI Design, Front-End Development, Interaction Design
Deliverables: Web App Design, Time-Tracking Interface, Local Storage Functionality, Wireframes

View Live Project

The Work Day Scheduler is a productivity-focused web app designed to help users efficiently plan and manage their daily tasks. The aim was to create a simple, visually clear tool that supports time-blocked scheduling while encouraging accountability and consistency throughout the workday.

Problem

Many professionals struggle with structuring their day effectively — switching between tasks, forgetting deadlines, and lacking visibility into what’s next. Existing planners are often cluttered or lack real-time interactivity, making daily organization more difficult than it should be.

Solution

The Work Day Scheduler was designed as a lightweight, intuitive daily planner that combines time-block scheduling with color-coded feedback. It helps users visualize their tasks by hour, save notes locally, and stay on track without external distractions.

Features

  • Dynamic Time Blocks: Hours are color-coded to show past, present, and future tasks in real-time.
  • Local Storage Integration: Tasks are automatically saved, allowing users to refresh or return later without data loss.
  • Simple UI: Clean interface that makes task entry effortless and visually distinct.
  • Responsive Layout: Optimized for use on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Impact

The Work Day Scheduler improves personal productivity by making task management visual and intuitive. It empowers users to stay organized, reduce mental clutter, and maintain focus throughout the day — supporting a balanced and efficient workflow.
