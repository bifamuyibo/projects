My Approach

A deeper look at my UX/UI design methodology

Research

I start every project by understanding users, their goals, and the market context. Through interviews, surveys, and competitor analysis, I gather insights that inform design decisions.

Ideation

Using sketches, wireframes, and flow diagrams, I explore multiple solutions, balancing creativity and usability. Collaboration with stakeholders ensures alignment early in the process.

Design

I create high-fidelity interfaces emphasizing clarity, accessibility, and visual hierarchy. Interaction design is considered at every step to ensure seamless user experience.

Testing & Iteration

Usability testing allows me to validate solutions and iterate rapidly. Feedback loops ensure the final product meets user needs and business goals.

Implementation & Handoff

I collaborate closely with developers, providing assets, documentation, and guidance to bring the design to life. I ensure the end product stays true to the design intent.
