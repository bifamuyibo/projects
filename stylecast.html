StyleCast, 2024

Role: Creative direction, UX/UI design, front-end development
Deliverables: Branding, User Research, Wireframes, Responsive Web App

View Live Project

From user research, I learned that people often check the weather before heading out — but they struggle to decide what to wear. For many, style is not only about looking good, but also about comfort, confidence, and practicality.

Problem

Existing weather apps focus heavily on data presentation but don’t bridge the gap between weather information and real lifestyle decisions.

Solution

StyleCast was designed as a digital companion that transforms weather forecasts into clear, actionable outfit suggestions.

Features

  • Weather & Outfit Pairing: Live forecasts with curated outfit suggestions.
  • Clothing Visuals: Clear visuals showing “sunny casual” or “rainy chic.”
  • Awareness: City input with instant weather + outfit ideas.
  • Personalisation: Refine recommendations to suit personal styles.

Impact

With StyleCast, checking the weather becomes an inspiring daily ritual. It helps users save time, reduce decision fatigue, and gain confidence in their appearance.
