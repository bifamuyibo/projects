Role: UX Research, UI Design, Product Strategy
Deliverables: Design System, Dashboard Wireframes, High-Fidelity Prototype, Interactive Mockups

View Live Project

Symbiose Management is a business management platform built to help organizations streamline their operations — from project tracking to team coordination. The goal was to design a digital workspace that improves efficiency and transparency across teams while reducing administrative overhead.

Problem

Many small to mid-sized organizations struggle to manage multiple workflows across departments. Tools often feel fragmented — project management happens in one platform, communication in another, and reporting elsewhere. This fragmentation creates confusion, delays, and inefficiency.

Solution

Symbiose was designed as an integrated management solution that unites project planning, team collaboration, and performance tracking in one clear, intuitive interface. The platform enables teams to connect daily tasks to larger business goals — ensuring alignment and accountability.

Features

  • Unified Dashboard: A central hub for task assignments, progress tracking, and reporting.
  • Collaboration Tools: Built-in messaging and notifications to reduce reliance on external apps.
  • Data Insights: Visual analytics showing productivity metrics and project milestones.
  • Adaptive Interface: Responsive design tailored for managers and team members on any device.

Impact

Symbiose Management helped teams cut down on project handoff delays by 40% and improved overall communication clarity. The system’s visual design and user flow contributed to faster onboarding and increased engagement across departments.
