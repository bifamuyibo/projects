FaithFlix Movie App, 2025

Role: Product Designer, UX/UI Designer, Front-End Developer
Deliverables: Branding, User Research, Wireframes, Responsive Web Application

View Live Project

FaithFlix is a Christian-focused streaming platform that curates inspiring movies and faith-based films for audiences seeking wholesome entertainment. The challenge was to design a digital experience that blends discovery, storytelling, and a sense of community within a modern interface.

Problem

Many faith-based audiences struggle to find streaming platforms that consistently offer wholesome, family-friendly content in an engaging way. Existing solutions often lack personalization, curation, or a modern user experience aligned with streaming standards.

Solution

FaithFlix was designed as an accessible, user-friendly streaming platform that focuses on curated Christian movies, inspirational films, and documentaries. The design emphasizes simplicity, clarity, and emotional connection while maintaining a premium, cinematic look.

Features

  • Curated Library: A handpicked selection of faith-based films with clear categories and search functionality.
  • Responsive Layout: Optimized for desktop and mobile for seamless viewing and exploration.
  • Visual Branding: A warm, minimal interface with a focus on emotional storytelling through imagery.
  • Instant Access: Users can view trailers and access streaming links directly.

Impact

FaithFlix delivers a focused and uplifting viewing experience for families and individuals seeking meaningful content. By merging faith, technology, and design, the platform provides a safe, engaging alternative to mainstream streaming services.
